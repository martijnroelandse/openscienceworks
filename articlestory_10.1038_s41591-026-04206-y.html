Full narrative

This study represents a significant advancement in Alzheimer’s disease research, focusing on the predictive capabilities of plasma p-tau217 biomarkers. Conducted by a collaboration of key institutions including the Banner Sun Health Research Institute and University College London, the work underscores the potential of plasma p-tau217 as a reliable biological clock for Alzheimer's disease onset. The backing of reputable funding agencies, such as the NIH and the Alzheimer's Association, further validates the importance of this research in the field.
