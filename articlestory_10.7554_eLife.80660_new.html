Full narrative

The dataset titled "A searchable image resource of Drosophila GAL4 driver expression patterns with single neuron resolution" represents a pivotal contribution to the field of neuroscience, focusing on Drosophila melanogaster as a model for studying neuronal activity. The dataset has been developed at the Janelia Research Campus, Howard Hughes Medical Institute, with backing from prestigious funding agencies, such as the NIH HHS and the Howard Hughes Medical Institute, indicating a robust institutional investment in the research area.

This work has generated substantial interest in the academic community, evidenced by its 150 citations in various influential venues, notably eLife, bioRxiv, and Nature.
