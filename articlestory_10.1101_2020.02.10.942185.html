Full narrative

Jaimes and colleagues' preprint on the structural modeling of the nCoV spike protein stands out due to its immediate relevance and scientific innovation, providing insights that differentiate it from the SARS-CoV family. This work has been recognized with 511 citations, showcasing its substantial impact on the field of virology and structural biology since its sharing on bioRxiv.

The preprint, which eventually transitioned to a peer-reviewed publication in a prominent journal, is a testament to the authors' commitment to open science practices. This backing, combined with the geographical footprint illustrated by citations from countries including the United States, United Kingdom, and Brazil, underscores the international significance of their findings.
