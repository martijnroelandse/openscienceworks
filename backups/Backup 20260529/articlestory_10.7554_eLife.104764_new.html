Full narrative

The work presented by Wolff et al. offers critical insights into the Drosophila central complex, emphasizing its role in neuropeptide expression and the regulation of sleep. The authors have effectively utilized innovative cell type-specific driver lines, which are foundational tools for dissecting the molecular and cellular mechanisms underlying these processes. The tools generated and the findings, which include characterization of neuromodulators used by different cells, will undoubtedly serve as a foundation for future studies of this brain region.
