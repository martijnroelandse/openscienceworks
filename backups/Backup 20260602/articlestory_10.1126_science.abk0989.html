Full narrative

The study on the adaptation of white clover to global urban environmental change represents a substantial contribution to ecological research, as evidenced by its 180 citations. Importantly, the work received funding from the Natural Environment Research Council, validating the significance of the research area and further supporting its credibility within the scientific community. With a citation landscape that includes prominent venues like the Journal of Ecology and Molecular Ecology, the findings are being actively integrated into ongoing ecological discourse.
