Full narrative

The research presented in "Global urban environmental change drives adaptation in white clover" (2022) has made a notable impact in the field of ecology, receiving 189 citations in scholarly literature. Such recognition underscores the significance of this study and highlights the ongoing discourse regarding urban environmental changes and their biological implications.

The collaborative effort from leading institutions, including Uppsala University and Stanford University, speaks to the quality and validity of the research. This institutional backing indicates a robust foundation for exploring how urban environments affect plant adaptability, specifically in the case of white clover. The endorsement from the Natural Environment Research Council further solidifies the research's importance and relevance in the context of environmental science.
